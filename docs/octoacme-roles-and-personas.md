# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA / Testing Lead

### Role Summary
QA/Testing Leads define and execute quality assurance strategies, validate acceptance criteria, and ensure software meets quality and compliance standards before release.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain test plans aligned with project scope and acceptance criteria
- Define testing approach (unit, integration, e2e, performance, security)
- Execute manual and automated testing and report defects
- Validate quality gates and define Definition of Done criteria
- Participate in planning and estimation for test effort
- Coordinate with developers on test coverage and automation needs
- Lead regression testing before releases

### Goals
- Catch quality issues early and prevent production incidents
- Maintain high standards for usability and performance
- Enable fast, confident releases through automated testing
- Support team in understanding and meeting acceptance criteria

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and daily standups
- Test plan and defect reports
- Release readiness sign-offs

---

## Technical Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide architecture guidance, ensure technical feasibility, mentor developers, and help mitigate technical risks.

### Responsibilities
- Review proposed solutions for technical feasibility and design quality
- Guide architecture decisions and technology trade-offs
- Mentor developers and conduct code reviews
- Identify and escalate technical risks and dependencies
- Participate in estimation and capacity planning
- Support definition of technical acceptance criteria and Definition of Done
- Ensure consistency with overall system architecture and best practices

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable, and performant solutions
- Build team capability and code quality
- Reduce technical debt and rework
- Enable sustainable delivery pace

### Typical Communication
- Technical design reviews and architecture discussions
- Code review feedback and mentoring
- Risk escalations and dependency coordination

---

## Stakeholder / Sponsor

### Role Summary
Stakeholders and Sponsors represent business priorities, provide executive oversight, approve scope and resource decisions, and communicate project status to leadership and customers.

### Responsibilities
- Define business objectives and success criteria
- Approve project charter, scope, and major changes
- Provide or secure necessary resources and budget
- Make or escalate key decisions (scope, timeline, trade-offs)
- Serve as liaison to leadership and customer groups
- Review project status and provide feedback
- Support removal of organizational blockers

### Goals
- Ensure project delivers business value and ROI
- Maintain alignment between project and organizational strategy
- Enable team to succeed through resource and sponsor support
- Communicate progress and manage expectations

### Typical Communication
- Monthly stakeholder reviews and status reports
- Decision sign-offs and approval gates
- Escalation handling and blocker resolution

---

## Security / Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
Security and Compliance Officers ensure projects meet security standards, compliance requirements, and organizational policies. They provide guidance on threat assessment, data protection, and incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Review project requirements for security and compliance implications
- Conduct threat assessments and security architecture reviews
- Define security controls and acceptance criteria
- Perform security testing and vulnerability assessments (or coordinate with vendors)
- Ensure compliance with relevant standards and regulations
- Participate in incident response and post-incident reviews
- Provide security guidance and training to developers

### Goals
- Prevent security breaches and data loss
- Maintain compliance with industry standards and regulations
- Build security-aware culture
- Enable confident, secure releases

### Typical Communication
- Security design reviews and threat modeling sessions
- Compliance checklists and audit documentation
- Incident escalations and post-mortem participation
- Security training and guidance
